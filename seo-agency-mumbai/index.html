The time it takes to begin noticing tangible results depends on your SEO campaign goals. For instance, it'll most likely take much longer to grow a site from scratch to prominence compared to helping an already established site rank for certain keywords.

On average, it takes between 4 - 12 months to begin noticing significant SEO improvements. Things like increase in traffic and page ranking usually happens within 6 months, but more bottom-funnel action like conversion and sales may only begin registering after about 6 months or more.

The reason why it might seem like a professional SEO company in Mumbai is taking quite long to generate results is the onerous requirements for creating lasting results. Google is increasingly tightening restrictions on shortcuts and hacks that could get quick results without any tangible value, forcing everyone to work hard in creating a value and an incredible user experience for searchers.

Tracking SEO results is one of the main sticking points of partnering up with SEO agencies. That's because there are so many metrics involved, and clients often disagree with agencies over which metrics to track.
The main metrics to track depends on the specific goals being targeted. For instance, if you want more social engagement, the metrics you need to track will be different compared to when you're trying to build domain authority.
The metrics that are most important to organic growth include:

  • Impressions
  • Clicks
  • Organic traffic
  • Page rankings
  • Domain authority
  • Page authority
  • Backlinks
  • Referral traffic from other sites
  • Conversion rates
